Welcome to the study!

  • In this study, we will provide you with pages that look like this. At the top you see a query (marked 1) and below it the audio clip of the passage retrieved for that query by a search system.
  • You will see 29 such pages in total.
  • For this study, you won't be able to change the query.
  • You can play the audio by pressing the play button (marked 2). You can also pause the audio by pressing the pause button (marked 3).
  • For each page, we ask you to judge whether the audio clip passage is actually relevant to the query. You can select between 3 judgments: relevant, somewhat relevant and not relevant.
  • You can also choose I do not know if you are unsure about the relevance of the passage.
  • The form to judge relevance will be shown once atleast half of the audio is played. However, we strongly encourage you to listen to the whole audio for this study.
  • Once you are satisfied with your assessment click the Next query button to go to the next page.
  • After you have gone through all 29 scenarios, we will take you to a final questionnaire to complete the study.
  • We request you to only use the passage we provide to assess the relevance. Please do not issue queries in a separate search engine.
  • Please avoid the use of your browser's back button as you go through this study.
  • When ready, type in your Prolific ID and click Submit to start the study!
