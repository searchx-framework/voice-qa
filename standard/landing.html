Welcome to the study!

  • You will be asked to walk through 29 search scenarios, one after the other.
  • For each scenario, we will present you with a query at the top of the page.
  • For this study, you won't be able to change the query.
  • We will provide you with the audio clip of one passage below the query. You can play the passage by pressing the play button. You can also pause the passage by pressing the pause button.
  • Your task is to listen to the passage and judge the relevance of the passage to the query.
  • You have to choose whether the passage is perfectly relevant, somewhat relevant or not relevant at all.
  • Once you are satisfied with your assessment click the Next query button to go to the next search scenario.
  • After you have gone through all 29 scenarios, we will take you to an exit survey to complete the study.
  • We request you to only use the passage we provide to assess the relevance. Please do not issue queries in a separate search engine.
  • Please avoid the use of your browser's back button as you go through this experiment.
  • When ready, type in your Prolific ID and click Submit to start the study!
