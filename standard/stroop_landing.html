Task 1

Welcome to Task 1!

  • For this first task, you will be shown several pages with a word presented at the top. Each word is written in a colour. You have to identify the colour in which the word is written, not what the word says.
  • Take a look at the example screenshot below. At the top, you see a word (e.g., Red, marked 1) written in the colour blue. Below it, there are four options (marked 2).
  • For this example, you should select blue from the list, as the colour the word is presented in is blue (although the word says Red). Once you select the correct option, a Next button will appear, taking you to the next step.
  • A total of 48 pages will be presented to you. Complete them as fast as you can.
  • Please do not undertake another activity while performing this task. You risk being disqualified from this study if you do.
  • When you are ready to begin, click the Start button below to begin.
